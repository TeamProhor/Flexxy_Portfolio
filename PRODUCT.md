# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Potential clients (brands, creators, agencies) looking to hire a highly skilled video editor and motion designer. They are evaluating technical mastery and aesthetic taste, seeking someone who can deliver premium, engaging video content.

## Product Purpose
A portfolio landing page that showcases the editor's best work, their process, and why they are the right choice for premium projects. The goal is to convert visitors into high-quality leads by evoking confidence, technical mastery, and inspiration. (Note: This is a portfolio, not a course.)

## Brand Personality
Premium, Sleek, Confident.
*References: Apple, Linear.*

## Anti-references
Cluttered SaaS dashboards, generic portfolios, cheap templates, overcrowded layouts.

## Design Principles
- **Show, don't tell**: Let the video and motion work take center stage. The UI should frame the work, not compete with it.
- **Premium restraint**: Use high contrast, smooth exponential easing, and deliberate, generous spacing to imply quality and expense.
- **Confidence in minimalism**: Avoid desperate marketing tropes (like artificial urgency, cluttered badges, or loud gradient text).
- **Expert pacing**: Reveal content sequentially and smoothly, mirroring the rhythm of a well-edited video.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA compliance. Maintain high contrast for all text. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` (replace complex transforms with simple crossfades for users who request it).
